Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. Make the topping
  4. Bake the mac and cheese
