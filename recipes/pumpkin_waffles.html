Pumpkin Waffles with Apple Cider Syrup

Description

A fall, winter and special occasion family favorite, these are fairly easy to make and delicious! They're sure to please even picky eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Apple Cyder Syrup

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, allspice, ginger, salt, and brown sugar in a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir the flour mixture and 1/4 cup melted butter to the pumpkin mixture, stirring just to combine.
  4. To make the syrup, stir together the sugar, cornstarch, and cinnamon in a saucepan.
